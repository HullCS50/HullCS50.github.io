Three Thing Game

If you want to take part in Three Thing Game during the competiton you can register here.

There is no need to come to Hull to take part, you will be able to pick your things and submit your entries and be judged using the magic of the interwebs.

You will pick your things on Wednedsay online and you cah work on them in the run up to judging on Saturday. You can work in the Famous Fenner Lab if you wish, we will have that open during the event.
