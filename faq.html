Frequently Asked Questions

When is it?

The event runs in Hull from the evening of Friday 10th of May to the end of Saturday 11th. If you fancy making a weekend of it you can then use Sunday to explore all your old haunts.

Where is it?

The social events will take place in Bronte Brasserie in the Canham Turner building (what you might remember as Staff House).

Three Thing Game will be running in it's spiritual home: the Fenner Computer Lab.

Who can come?

The event is open past or present students or staff members over the fifty years of the Computer Science Department in Hull. You can contact us here

What's happening?

The event will formally start on Friday 16th of May at 6:00pm with a meetup at the Bronte Brasserie in Canham Turner (what you might remember as Staff House) starting at 6:00 pm. A good place to meet up and unwind after your journey up to Hull. We'll be around to chat about the old times.

On Saturday morning our day of celebration starts at 11:00am with tours around the campus, or a coffee in the Bronte Brasserie. We'll have a memory board for pictures from the past (send us some if you want to be famous-ish) plus retro consoles to play on. After lunch we'll have a sequence of micro-talks, starting with an overview of current research activities and then moving onto stories from the department past and present. If you've got a story to tell we'd love to hear from you.

At 4:30 we'll award the prizes for the Three Thing Game competition and serve out the food. Then, at 7:00pm we start the evening's entertainments with a 'Welcome Party Redux'. If you've got fond memories of your first social gathering in the department this is your chance to relive them. We're going to have a Welcome Quiz with fiendish questions, retro games, slot car racing we'll even 3D scan you and create an image of you in carbonite.

(this program is slightly provisional and may be subject to change - especially if we can't find any carbonite)

What about Three Thing Game?

Glad you asked. We'll be running a special Three Thing Game hackathon over the weekend. You'll can form a team and take part making a game. There'll be a team sign up for the Three Thing Game completiton and we will run an online Thing Auction on Wednesday 8th May where you can get your things. You can spend the rest of the week preparing and then you can turn up on Friday and get together and code up your masterpiece. Judging will take place on Saturday afternoon starting at 3:00 pm with a grand prize presentation at the celebration at 4:30 pm.

Will there be anything for kids?

Yes. If you want to bring your kids along and show off your programming smarts by putting one of our Hull Pixelbot robots through its paces (and even make it play as part of a robot rugby team) we'll have a robot arena and some robots ready for you to play with on Saturday morning.

How much does it cost?

We will be providing food and drink for the evening entertainment and so we will be making a charge of 10 pounds per person to cover this. This price is subject to change (it may go down) if we can attract more sponsorship.

Can I help?

That would be lovely. We will be recruiting 'celebration elves' to help run the retro games, carbonite printing, slot car racing, Pixelbot rugby and campus tours. If you are local to Hull and fancy giving up a bit of your time we'd love to hear from you. Anyone who helps for an hour or so will get free entry to the event. You can contact us here

Can I sponsor something?

Yes please. We would love to talk to you about sponsorship. For a slot on our sponsors banner to paying for drinks, and everything in between. You can contact us here
